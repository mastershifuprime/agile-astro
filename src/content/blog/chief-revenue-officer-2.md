In this follow-up interview, we dive deeper into Merick's strategies for scaling revenue.

## Revenue Strategy Deep Dive

### Q: What's your approach to scaling revenue?

A: "It starts with understanding your ideal customer profile deeply. We invest heavily in customer research and use those insights to guide our go-to-market strategy."

### Q: How do you balance growth with profitability?

A: "We focus on efficient growth. Every dollar spent on customer acquisition should generate predictable returns. We track unit economics religiously."
